The Drivers of Blue

Chris Chartron

Chris can grow facial hair in an amazing number of configurations. He can install a hard wood floor in the time it takes the average person to drive to Home Depot, and he is always a threat on the track.

JOHN DOHRMANN

From the gritty and tough world of advertising, John’s knowledge of racing comes from what he sees on TV. Look for him to take F1-style lines and to expect three-second pit stops.

Austin Fadel

Austin was the breakout talent in Blue’s initial testing session. Don’t bet against this young rookie to lay down the best times of the race. His secret: not sleeping the night before a race.

Chris Gunn

Gunner has more car control talent in his fingernail than most people have in their whole finger. Look for Chris to find the edge of Blue’s capabilities, go over that edge, and then come back in spectacular fashion.

Charlie Jorgensen

Some call him the Car-Whisperer for his ability to effortlessly coax epic performance out of unlikely machines. Charlie’s smooth driving style was on full display during testing, and it was the envy of pit lane.

Mike Pricer

Between 2009 and 2011 Mike carefully crafted his aggressive driving style at a private underground track in Abu Dhabi. For the last year Mike has been getting reacclimated to sunlight and laying down blistering times around the world.
