Building a Giant X-MEN Sentinel

How it All Began... Thanks Hasbro

The Post

I was scrolling through the Action Figure Junkies Facebook Group and I noticed a giant Sentinel Leg. I didn't think much of it, I figured Hasbro or some fan was messing around but I did take note of the size. Next thing I knew I was modeling and printing a giant sentinel head, but it wasn't good enough, I sought to make something huge on my little Elegoo Mars 3D printer. I began looking through the 1/6 scale statues from Sideshow Collectibles and Iron Studios. I wanted to base my design for the body off of the Sideshow Statue and use the head from Iron Studios to inspire the head.

The Announcement

On July 10th Hasbro Pulse & HasLab announced they were crowdfunding a Master Mold Sentinel here. This inspired me to take my project to the next level. My plan, process, and execution can be seen throughout the hobby section of my site, or you can click here to start the journey.
